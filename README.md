## 02 · Typography

| Role | Font | Size | Weight |
|---|---|---|---|
| Headline | Georgia (serif) | 26–32px | Bold |
| Category label | Sans-serif | 10–11px | Medium · ALL CAPS |
| Body text | Sans-serif | 13–14px | Regular |
| Stat / Number | Georgia (serif) | 32–48px | Bold |

---

## 03 · Post Templates

### A · Knowledge Card
- Background: Sand `#F5F0E8`
- Category label top-left in Burnt Coral
- Serif headline center
- Short body text below
- @EngrCivil_ bottom-left, small and muted
- Format: 1:1 square

### B · Stat / Observation (Dark)
- Background: Graphite `#1A1A18`
- Category label top-left in Burnt Coral
- Large number in Burnt Coral (serif)
- Short headline in Sand below the number
- @EngrCivil_ bottom-left, muted
- Format: 1:1 square

### C · Steps / List
- Background: Sand `#F5F0E8`
- Category label top-left in its category color
- Numbered steps: 01 · 02 · 03 in Burnt Coral serif
- Each step: short label + one line description
- @EngrCivil_ bottom-left, muted
- Format: 4:5 portrait (Instagram)

## 05 · Design Rules

- **R01** — Always use Sand `#F5F0E8` or pure black as background. Never random colors.
- **R02** — Every post gets a category label in ALL CAPS at the top in its accent color.
- **R03** — Headlines are always serif (Georgia). Body text is always sans-serif.
- **R04** — Numbers and stats are always large, bold, and in Burnt Coral.
- **R05** — @EngrCivil_ always appears at the bottom, small and muted. Never prominent.
- **R06** — Maximum 2 accent colors per post.
- **R07** — Step numbers (01, 02, 03) use serif font in accent color. Never plain bullets.
- **R08** — Keep it spacious. White space is part of the design.

---

## 06 · How to Use This With Claude

Paste this at the start of any post generation prompt:

> Follow my design system. Background: Sand #F5F0E8. Headline: serif, bold, Graphite. Category label: ALL CAPS, small, in the category accent color. Stats/numbers: large, bold, Burnt Coral #E8654A. Handle @EngrCivil_ at the bottom, small and muted. Keep it spacious with generous whitespace.

Then add your topic. Example:

> Topic: 3 things a QC inspector checks before concrete pour. Use the Steps template.
